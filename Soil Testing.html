SOIL TESTING SERVICE



We bring solutions to make life easier for our clients

  • Soil testing in construction is the process of analyzing soil samples to determine its properties and suitability for supporting structures. It helps engineers assess factors like soil strength, compaction, moisture content, and composition, ensuring the soil can bear the load of the building. The tests guide decisions on foundation design, prevent structural failures, and optimize construction techniques. Key tests include soil classification, compaction, bearing capacity, and permeability. Proper soil testing is crucial for the safety, stability, and longevity of any construction project.


Types of Soil Test



  • Direct Shear

  • Standard Proctor Test

  • Modified Proctor Test

  • California Bearing Ratio

  • Consolidation

  • Falling Head Permeability

  • Constant Head Permeability

  • Unconfined Compression

  • Specific gravity

  • Vane Shear

  • Dry Density

  • Atterberg limit

  • Moisture Content

  • Tri-Axial test
